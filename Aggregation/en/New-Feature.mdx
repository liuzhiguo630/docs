### Multi-Voice Reading

After activating the button in the input box, each reply from the model will generate emotionally rich multi-voice reading audio

Or you can click the multi-voice reading button after a reply to generate audio for a specific reply

![Multi Voice Jp](/images/Aggregation/New-Feature/multi-voice.jpg)

<Note>
  This feature will incur additional credit consumption. If you only want to listen to audio, you can also choose the cheaper single-voice reading feature
</Note>

![Single Vioce Jp](/images/Aggregation/New-Feature/single-vioce.jpg)

### AI Real-time Background Image Generation

After enabling this feature, the chat background will generate background images that match the context in real-time based on AI's replies

Giving you a more immersive conversation experience with AI

![Ai Image Gi](/images/Aggregation/New-Feature/ai-image.gif)

### Auto-Advance Plot

After enabling this feature, AI will predict the next few plot directions when interacting with you and provide you with several recommended buttons

Clicking the button will send the corresponding request to AI to automatically advance the plot

![Auto Movie Gi](/images/Aggregation/New-Feature/auto-movie.gif)

### Multi-Model Selection

After clicking this button, you can select up to 5 models at the same time and request their replies simultaneously

Choose the one you're most satisfied with!

![More Requests Gi](/images/Aggregation/New-Feature/more-requests.gif)
